Upload Guidelines

Only upload materials you have permission to share

Ensure you have the right to distribute the syllabus. Do not upload copyrighted materials without permission.

Use the correct course information

Double-check course codes, names, and professor information to ensure accuracy.

Keep reviews constructive and respectful

Share your honest experience while maintaining a respectful tone towards professors and fellow students.

Focus on course content and structure

Provide insights about the syllabus, assignments, exams, and overall course organization.
