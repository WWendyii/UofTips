What is UofTips?

UofTips is a student-created digital platform, which is a personalized student assistant platform designed to support your academic life at UofT. It uses behavior data to optimize your learning experience.

The Problem

  • Scattered course information across multiple platforms
  • Difficulty finding past syllabi
  • Lack of professor comparison tools
  • Course selection feels like a "mystery box"

Our Solution

  • Centralized course information hub
  • Syllabus library with consent-based uploads
  • Professor comparison features
  • AI-powered recommendations

Suggestions

Based on your activity, like frequently visited resources, performance trends, and browsing habits, UofTips tailors recommendations for study tips, courses, and events.
